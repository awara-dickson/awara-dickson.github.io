Who I am

Hello! I'm Dickson, a passionate front-end engineer and UI/UX designer. I specialize in crafting seamless, user-friendly experiences with a keen eye for design and attention to detail. With expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS, I bring landing pages and interfaces to life, focusing on clean, responsive code and smooth interactivity.

In addition to front-end development, I leverage Figma to design and prototype commercial websites, ensuring each element aligns with the brand and meets user needs. Whether it's developing from scratch or refining an existing concept, my goal is to create interfaces that are both functional and visually compelling.

I'm excited to share my journey and showcase the projects I've crafted along the way. Let's connect and bring more ideas to life!
